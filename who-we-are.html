FBK Markets is a proudly South African brokerage built to empower traders through transparency, technology, and trust.

We are more than a trading platform — we are a community of over 450 000 clients worldwide, united by one purpose: to make trading fair, simple, and rewarding for everyone.

Operating as a Juristic Representative of RocketX (Pty) Ltd (FSP 52142) under the supervision of the Financial Sector Conduct Authority (FSCA), FBK Markets provides regulated access to global financial markets through dependable technology, competitive conditions, and strong client protection.

Since our founding, we’ve processed over R64 million in withdrawals, proving our ongoing commitment to transparency, efficiency, and delivering real value to our traders.

Our Mission

To make trading accessible to everyone by combining world-class tools, education, and transparent service that help traders grow with confidence.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognised brokerage that connects traders to the markets through innovation, reliability, and long-term relationships built on trust.
