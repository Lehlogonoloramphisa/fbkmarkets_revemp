FICA Identification

Understand the documents we need to verify your FBK Markets profile and keep your account compliant.

Accepted FICA Documents and Checklist

The Financial Intelligence Centre Act (FICA) requires financial institutions, such as FBK Markets, to establish and verify clients' identities to combat money laundering and the financing of terrorism. The documentation set out below allows us to comply with these obligations and protect your trading account.

Please supply the following documents for your application:

  • Proof of identity
  • Proof of bank account
  • Proof of residence

Proof of Identity

As an FBK Markets client, you are able to trade before you submit any required documents; however, it is mandatory that all documents are submitted before any withdrawals are processed.

Smart ID Card

The Smart Card is South Africa’s latest version of the ID document. It is a card-style form of identification designed for user convenience while retaining key security features.

  • Make sure all four corners of the document are clearly visible within the frame.
  • The names on the ID document must match the names on your FBK Markets profile.
  • The date of birth must correspond with the details captured on your profile.

Green ID Book

The traditional green ID book remains valid and shares many features with the Smart ID. Submit the photo and detail pages in full colour.

  • Ensure every page is unobstructed and easy to read.
  • The ID book must show at least two coat of arms stamps.
  • Confirm that the full names and date of birth match your trading profile.

Passport

A valid passport can be used to verify your identity. Standard passports contain your full name, photograph, place and date of birth, signature and expiration date.

  • The names on the passport must match those listed on your profile.
  • Check that the passport is still valid; most passports expire after ten years.
  • The date of birth must match the information captured on your FBK Markets profile.
  • Include the verification markers at the bottom of the passport page showing the name, passport number and ID number.

Proof of Bank Accounts

Proof of banking is official communication from a regulated financial institution confirming that you hold a valid account to be used for FBK Markets transactions.

Acceptable documents include a bank confirmation letter or a stamped bank statement issued within the last three months. These statements list the account holder’s name, residential address, account number and branch details, plus a summary of balances, deposits, refunds and withdrawals.

Bank confirmation letters work on the same principle and must clearly display your full name as it appears on your ID, the account number and an untampered bank stamp with the issue date.

Proof of Residence

Proof of residence confirms where you live. The document must show your full name and physical address. In line with the Financial Intelligence Centre Act, FBK Markets must keep accurate and up-to-date records for every client.

Document validity requirements

  • Monthly issued documents must be less than three months old.
  • Annually issued documents must be dated within the last year.
  • The document must display the suburb or township name together with the ERF or street address.
  • Documents showing only a postal address are not acceptable.
  • Electronic records are permitted if the sender’s full details are visible.
  • If the proof of residence shows a third party’s name, submit a completed cohabitation declaration with supporting documents.

What qualifies as proof of residence?

  • Utility bills such as municipal water, electricity or managing agent statements.
  • Policy or medical aid statements with the policy number visible.
  • Traffic fine statements issued by a Metro Police station (excluding e-toll).
  • Letters from your municipal councillor.
  • Tax certificates.
  • Recent rental agreements or active leases.
  • Municipal rates and taxes invoices.
  • Statements from National Credit Regulator registered providers.
  • Security service bills from PSIRA registered providers.
  • Approved SARS documents.
  • Television licence renewal or confirmation letters.
  • Subscription television account statements such as MultiChoice.
  • Official employer letters for staff residing on company premises.
  • Formal registration letters from a college, university or technical institution.
