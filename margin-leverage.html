FBK Markets Leverage

Leverage involves borrowing capital to magnify exposure. By offering leverage from 1:100 to 1:1000, we give you control over how far you wish to risk. Combine our flexible tiers with a minimum deposit of R100, ultra-low spreads and agile execution to unlock larger trading opportunities.

Leverage Risk

High leverage can accelerate profits but it can also compound losses. Always apply sound risk management, size positions carefully and monitor exposure: the higher the leverage, the greater the stakes.

Margin

Margin is the collateral required to maintain open trades. With a 1% margin, for example, you can control $100 000 in currency with just $1 000. Use leverage wisely to balance opportunity against capital preservation.

Stop-Out Level

If equity drops dramatically, our system will begin closing positions once the stop-out level of 30% is reached. This automatic protection helps prevent deeper losses when markets move against you.

Margin Monitoring and Call

Monitor used and free margin in real time, your equity is always visible in the trading platform.

Should your equity fall below 50%, we will attempt to notify you with a margin call. To stay in the trade, consider adding funds or closing positions manually; the choice remains yours.

High Risk Investment Warning

Trading forex and CFD's on margin carries a high level of risk and may not be suitable for all investors. The high degree of leverage can work against you as well as for you. Before deciding to trade forex and CFD's you should carefully consider your investment objectives, level of experience, and risk appetite. The possibility exists that you could sustain a loss of some or all your initial investment and therefore you should not invest money that you cannot afford to lose. You should be aware of all the risks associated with forex and CFD trading and seek advice from an independent financial advisor if you have any doubts.
